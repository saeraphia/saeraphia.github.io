About FindIt Portal

FindIt Portal is a web-based platform for students living in hostel dormitories and studying within campus grounds. The portal serves as a convenient and easy-to-access centralized digital hub where students can create a report on missing belongings, post inquiries about their own missing valuables, and connect each other to facilitate safe return of lost items.

By providing a dedicated space for missing items and providing a structured and organised system, FindIt Portal makes it easy and reliable for missing items reunited with their rightful owners. This application is built with simplicity and accessibility for all students, making sure the portal is user friendly, regardless of anyone’s technical background.
